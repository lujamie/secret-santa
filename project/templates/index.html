Welcome to Secret Santa!

You've landed on our Secret Santa page! If you were referred here by a friend, ask them for a party code so you can join their party. If you're not here to join a party, why not make one yourself? Invite friends and family to join you!

Once you're in a party, feel free to explore this site further. Make a wishlist... and then check out the wishlist of the person you've been assigned! Add a hint to help the person you've been assigned to guess who you are.

And once the end date of the party comes, be sure to look at the hint provided to you, and swap gifts in your party!

But most importantly, happy holidays! Enjoy the season, and spread cheer and kindness through your Secret Santa party!
